Mega Mission Media — Who I Am & What I Do

We are a creative media and production company.

I'm a one-person, full-service shop based in Fort Worth, Texas — and what I do comes down to two things: I help you run your business better, and I help you tell its story.

That means team assessments, process and change management, and back office support on one side — and Ride & Share interviews, website builds, podcasting, and social content on the other. One person. Every angle. Built in a car, born in obedience.

What I Do — 01

Business Advisory & Consulting

25+ years building teams, fixing broken processes, and getting businesses organized from the inside out.

Team Structure & Assessment

I help you build the org chart.

I interview your team and bring you the real feedback — what they're saying about you, your business, and where things are breaking down — so you know exactly how to pivot.

Process & Change Management

Stop doing it the hard way.

Twenty-five years of corporate process transformation, brought to your business. We find the bottleneck, fix it, and manage the change so it sticks.

Project Management & Back Office Support

Someone who keeps it all moving.

Scoping, timelines, workflow automation, Google Workspace setup, and virtual admin support — the operations layer that keeps your business running clean while you focus on the vision. Agile and Scrum methodology available.

What I Do — 02

Media & Digital Presence

This is what makes it visible. Your story, your brand, your business — captured, produced, and put on every screen that matters.

Signature Service

Ride & Share Interview

The front seat is for sale.

Your story. My car. Your asset. We capture your interview in a moving vehicle, produce it to broadcast quality, and hand you a video that's fully yours to use anywhere.

Website Build

Your digital home, built to last.

Custom, mobile-first sites in your brand voice — with SEO foundations and booking built in. Builds, refreshes, and maintenance available.

Social Media & Profiles

AI-powered. Brand-driven. Consistently you.

Monthly content packages plus profile optimization across LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram — so every platform represents you well.

Podcasting

Your studio is wherever you are.

No studio needed — we come to you, record, edit, and publish. We'll teach you to run it yourself if you'd rather.
